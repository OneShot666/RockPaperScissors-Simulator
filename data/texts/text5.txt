Glossary---
Behavior : What the entity is currently doing.

Chase : To go after the nearest 'prey' to defeat it.

Defeat : To defeat an entity is to transform it into its own type.

Dumb : Entity will act without any advanced strategy, just looking for nearest prey.

Entity : The object that moves in the simulation.

Flee : To go to the opposite direction of the closest predator (only in smart mode).

Simulation : A battle between entities.

Smart : For now, allow the smart entities to flee if no prey is found.

Toroidal : Sort of infinite map, if you go to the left, you'll end up to the right. And so on for up and down.

Type : The 'species' of the entity, basically rocks, papers and scissors.

Win : An entity wins when it's the last type standing in the simulation.
